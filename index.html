In Metro Manila, millions of people use public transportation like MRT/LRT, jeepneys, buses, taxis and tricycles to travel daily to work, school and some other places.

Many commuters got confused especially those who are not familiar with the place. Barker.ph is a website that will provide crowdsourced travel directions and mapping to all commuters. This is provides commuting guides and accessible information for commuters to reach places within and outside of Metro Manila and address other prevalent issues with commuting.This online commute portal will serve as a huge source of easily accessible information for commuters regarding public transportation and cost. This web portal will also provide a detailed commuting guides and step by step procedures of reaching places within and outside Metro Manila. Furthermore, this will be an efficient means of communication for all commuters as they will be able to know on what route to take, how much the fare is and what mode of transportation to ride.

This online portal will definitely address rampant issues pertaining to public transportation support system. Moreover, this will give an opportunity to acquaint oneself with commuter' s information.Additionally, the web portal will include features like providing information by allowing them to share events in real-time, give guides and instructions to reach specific locations, and share comments and suggestions like sending alerts and warnings about what' s happening in a specific locality, tweeting about traffic and other public transportation issues.
